【Navigating the Market: How to Choose a Men's Necklace】

Understanding Pearl Type and Quality

Distinguishing Real from Imitation Pearls

Natural Pearls: Extremely rare, 100% naturally created in mollusk.

Cultured Pearls: Created by surgically placing an irritant, making up most of today's market.

Imitation Pearls: Glass beads steeped in fish scales; can be tested by rubbing against real pearls (imitation glides, cultured feels gritty).

Key Quality Indicators for Pearls

Luster: Should be able to see yourself, surface blemish-free.

Shape: Perfectly symmetrical is rare; most baroque pearls are freshwater.

Size: Average cultured pearls are 7 to 7 1/2 millimeters in diameter.

Color & Strand Quality: Complement your complexion; strands should not bunch or twist, color should match.
